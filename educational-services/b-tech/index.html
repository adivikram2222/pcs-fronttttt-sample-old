M-TECH

The sole purpose behind crafting the MTech course is to produce research engineers who will get positions of “Technologist” in the industries and research institutes. This course will emphasize on practical exposure of conceptual knowledge. 

Master of Technology

M Tech full form is Master of Technology . M Tech is a postgraduate program that comes under the discipline of technology. This master program comes up with a duration of 2 years consisting of 4 semesters. M.Tech course focuses mainly on the implementation of existing technologies and gives gateway towards technical research. M.Tech course has tremendous specialization according to one's undergraduate degree in engineering undergraduate. Anyone who has graduated with a bachelor's degree in Engineering or Technology is eligible to take up this course. Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Data Science, Mechatronics, Software Engineering, etc are some of the demanding master's degrees in the market.

M.Tech Course Fee in India:

The average fee for the M Tech fees ranges from INR 25,000 to 2 lakh per annum. Which slightly vary according to universities/colleges which one wants to study. 

M.Tech Salary in India:

The salary varies according to the specialization and industry one is working. The average salary for M.Tech graduates is 7 lakh per annum. If one has relevant skills and experience, the salary package will be much higher.

M-Tech Eligibility

M-Tech Eligibility Criteria

To begin your higher studies in MTech, you need to fulfill the following requirements:

  • You must possess a B.E./B.Tech degree from a recognized college or university with a valid GATE score.
  • Those students who have completed the 5-years course in Science or Applied science can also apply for a Master of Technology course.
  • The biggest factor that helps students to get admission to the MTech program is their ranking in the entrance exam (GATE) or the other institution entrance test.
  • Applicants who have cleared M.Sc degree in Science/Mathematics/Statistics/Computer Applications or equivalent can also apply.
  • MCA aspirants are likewise qualified to apply for specializations accessible in sections of software engineering designing.

Note: Eligibility criteria, of course, may vary from institute to institute

GATE Exam is more concentrated on fundamental concepts. You should be excellent in the syllabus of your graduation. The GATE Syllabus focuses on 3 sections:

  • Engineering Mathematics
  • General Aptitude
  • Subject Questions

Mostly, colleges and universities offer admission on the basis of the GATE score. This exam is conducted by 7 IITs and IISc (Bangalore). It is an online examination of 3 hours consisting of 65 questions in total. One needs to go through with the syllabus and prepare well for these examinations.
